Culture

Did you know that Peru is a country which produces one of the best gastronomies in the world?

Have you ever been part of a culinary experience within a rural community? We know that in Peru there are located more than ten thousand rural communes, each of them providing its own unique gastronomy for uncountable years now. Could you imagine being part of these ancient collectives?

We believe that in order to preserve the past millennial we must ensure the future of these communities. Gastronomic knowledge has always represented a fundamental part of their culture in the past. It is therefore our vision to revalue the culinary abundance of these villages on a global level through our culinary experience that aims to integrate tourists applying our three pillars: biodiversity, culture and living together with the chosen community for three days. This will allow tourists to get to know different kinds of typical dishes and eventually to make possible that the commune will be able to offer their products with an additional value. For this purpose, we will advise them concerning the generation of their projects.

We want you to be part of this wonderful cycle, we want to bring you to an experience that interconnects culture and nature as one single sustainable unity. Your direct contact with the aliments as they are sowed, harvested, prepared and returned to the earth will be the key to understand millennial knowledge of nature and the communities which enable you to get to know your importance in this cycle.
