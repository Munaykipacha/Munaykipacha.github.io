Be a Explorer

In this phase the tourist will be able to live the experience of connecting their senses and existence with the environment that surrounds them. They will take a walk around the community, they will have the opportunity to feel part of many activities. As an explorer, you will also discover unique cave paintings enjoying a short trek and then closing the day appreciating a starlit sky and listening to the mystical experiences of the community.

Be a Gatherer

Through this phase, you will experiences a connection with biodiversity, for this, they will be taught cultivation and fishing techniques. They will also be able to participate as a ‘citizen scientist for a day’, helping to collect data and photographs by observing and counting macro and micro species, in order to contribute to future scientific research.The tourist will receive a class on compost, It also seeks to participate in the rescue of traditional medicine in conjunction with the inhabitants and finally the day will close by relaxing in the hot springs.

Be a Creative

In the last phase we want to teach you the power of the transformation, the inputs that nature offers us, being able to use their creativity. You will learn to make a Pachamanca by yourself together with the residents to appreciate the art (variety, flavor and color) and the culture that this preparation entails. After enjoying this experience, a retribution will be made to the land by collecting the waste from the food, preparing them and taking them to be composted according to the lessons of the previous day.

We will close the culinary retreat presenting the results of the observations of all the groups in order that they feel that they are part of a joint investigation and that they are a great team on the way to discover something.
